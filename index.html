Our Services

Data Visualisation

Data is a powerful tool to any business. Taking millions of lines of data and telling the right story is our business. We believe an organisation should spend their time capitalising on trends, insights and making important decisions that boost customer engagement and save costs; not tackling spreadsheets and databases.

  • Interactive Dashboards
  • Forecasting and Analysis
  • Reporting and Graphing

Software Development

Capturing an organisations data is key to understanding your customers needs and experience, and improving productivity and staff management. We believe that non-intrusive and easy-to-use lightweight tools are key to capturing and creating robust, accurate datasets.

  • Logging tools
  • Single-page applications
  • Online & Web applications
  • Lightweight desktop applications

Business Intelligence

Getting the right message to the right people in an organisation can be crucial. Business Intelligence assures that each department gets the data and reports they need that feeds into the bigger picture. Creating a self-serving reporting suite is important for reliability and timely reporting.

  • Report scheduling
  • Dashboard and web portal hosting
  • Automated self-serve reporting

Process Improvement

Reports and data can give important insights into your organisations internal processes. Identifying key markers for improvement and predicting the impact of changes are an important part of continuing improvement. Planning ahead and identifying these points can avoid uncertainty and reduce the risk of change..

  • Identifying areas for improvement
  • Tracking performance of changes
  • Predicting impact of changes
